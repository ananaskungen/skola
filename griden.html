Why you should start learning to code

The 21st century is a very interesting time period to live in. We've been introduced to the internet, mobile phones with touch screens, electric vehicles. The 21st century is really starting ona great edge for technology and learning to code is an essential skill that will be very helpful in the future

Why I think you shouldn't quit coding

When you first start to learn programing, you find yourself in a situation where things really aren't going your way and you start finding excuses on why you should not program or why "this might not be for me". Many people really wanna learn and something they keep forgetting is that they are learning, they are just not acknowledging that they are learning it. When they first start coding they get introduced to HTML & CSS and Javascript they learn different syntaxes and something they don't realize is that a week before they started learning code they had no clue what these syntaxes were or what they meant, so what I can say is, you are learning, it's just not at the pace you want to learn at. And it's okay, us humans are lazy, we like being lazy and we like things to go quick. So it's completely understandable and with coding you just have to give it time. Eventually you will learn. As with anything else.

What You Need to Change

So it's very easy to start coding, but once you're pushed against the wall, you decide to break, instead of breaking the wall down. The key to succeed in this section or in any job is to have the passion for it. And I mean eat, sleep & drink code repeat. Solving issues might sound scary and very hard, but the satisfaction and the relief of sitting there hours on end and problem-solving or study code is just an amazing feeling you get isnide, when you get something to work it's such a relief that cannot be explained. And I think that's something many peolpe don't realize, when you put in the hours and you're on the edge of getting it right, and you decide to leave is the worst thing a programmer can do. You need to understand that things take time and miracles do come true.
